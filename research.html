Research Areas

The TSL’s missions carry strong research components; in the course of designing, building, testing, and operating small satellites, the TSL seeks to develop innovative solutions that advance the state of the art in:

  • - The use of fractionated spacecraft to cooperatively accomplish complex mission objectives
  • - The use of small satellites to improve space domain awareness
  • - Orbital debris mitigation using small satellites
  • - Multi-satellite formation flying
  • - Low-thrust propulsion for orbital maneuvers and stationkeeping
  • - Attitude determination and control under strict size, power, and budget constraints
  • - Modular hardware and software design that reduces mission development cost, time, and risk
